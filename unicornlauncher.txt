Unicorns are mythical creatures that are often depicted as a horse with a single, spiraling horn on their forehead. They have been a part of folklore and mythology for centuries, and are often associated with purity, magic, and grace.

In popular culture, unicorns are often portrayed as symbols of femininity and beauty, and are often associated with rainbows and glitter. They are also popular in children's literature and media, often appearing in stories and cartoons as magical and whimsical creatures.

Despite their mythical status, many people are fascinated by unicorns and the symbolism they represent. Some people even believe that unicorns were real creatures that once roamed the earth, and there are many stories and legends about encounters with these magical beings.
